A note from Sean Hi, I'm Sean — and I built this app to solve a problem I live with every day.

After several traumatic brain injuries during my time in the military, I struggle to remember the things that matter most — names, important dates, the people I care about. I tried writing things down, but I'd forget where I wrote them.

So I built the tool I wished existed: automatic reminders, tied to a smart Amazon link that helps me find a thoughtful gift based on what the person actually loves. No more guessing. No more forgetting.

If you've ever missed a birthday and felt terrible about it, if you're juggling a full life, or if your memory isn't what it used to be — this is for you too. I hope it brings you closer to the people who matter.

Frequently asked questions
How does RecallDate work?

Add the people you care about along with their important dates — birthdays, anniversaries, graduations, anything you want to remember. Two weeks before each date, we'll send you an email with a personalized Amazon gift suggestion based on their interests and your budget.

When will I get reminders?

Reminders arrive by email exactly 14 days before each date. You'll also see upcoming events in the app up to 60 days ahead on the Upcoming tab.

Why 14 days before?

Two weeks is the sweet spot — long enough to order, ship, and thoughtfully wrap a gift without rushing. It gives you time to find something meaningful rather than settling for whatever's available last minute.

How do the gift suggestions work?

Each reminder includes a smart Amazon search link built from the person's relationship to you, their interests, and your set budget. Click the orange "Find gift on Amazon" button and Amazon shows relevant options instantly. You still pick the gift — we just help you find it fast.

Is my data private?

Yes. We show no ads, sell no data, and share nothing with third parties. Your account is private to you — your people, dates, notes, and budgets all belong to you alone.

How much does RecallDate cost?

Nothing. RecallDate is free and ad-free forever. If you find it valuable, the tip banner on the Upcoming tab lets you Venmo us a few bucks to help cover server costs — but there's no obligation.

Can I use this on my phone?

Yes! Open recalldate.com in Safari or Chrome on your phone, then tap Share → Add to Home Screen. It'll install like a native app with its own icon on your home screen.

How do I edit or delete someone?

Go to the People tab and tap any person's card. You'll see an edit window where you can update their info, add more dates, remove dates, or delete the person entirely.

What if my reminder email goes to spam?

First-time emails from new senders can occasionally land in spam filters. If that happens, mark it "Not Spam" and future reminders will arrive in your inbox. You can also add reminders@recalldate.com to your contacts to make sure they always come through.

I found a bug or have a feature idea. How do I reach you?

We'd love to hear from you! Send feedback to the email that sent your reminder (reminders@recalldate.com) and we'll read every message.
