You get two email reminders for every event. The first arrives 14 days before the date - enough time to browse Amazon and have the gift delivered. The second arrives on the actual day so you still have a chance to send a text or make a call.
14 days lines up with standard Amazon shipping windows. Order something the day you get the reminder and it will arrive with time to spare. The day-of reminder is a backup for sending a text or making a call.
